About Builder

CRC, today, focuses on providing the finest residential premises. Adhering to its core objective of providing reliability, quality, and innovation, the group offers different solutions in the real estate market. Our professionals and well-skilled staff members are undisputed leaders in the sector of real estate and they are trained to bring in the best opportunities for our clients and partners.

The forerunner of CRC and Chandgi Ram Nawal Kishore (CRNK) was established by Shri Kidar Nath Bansal in 1985 with a vision to instigate complete trust, transparency, and commitment to services through sanitary ware business. CRNK holds a robust association with popular brand names like Kohler, Supreme, Jaquar, Hindware, and much more.

Taking this well-skilled experience forward with an objective of delivering what’s promised to the clients, CRC was incepted in 2006. The base of CRC was oriented by the Managing Director Mr. Satish Garg, the prime pillar of CRNK
